ABOUT

Hello, my name is Chantal and I'm an aspiring graphic designer and illustrator currently studying at Seminole State College. I have been drawing and painting for as long as I can remember and am working towards making it a career for myself. I used to love concept art and more illustrative type of works but have transitioned to creating more graphics. I try my best to learn a wide variety of mediums and styles to make myself as versatile as possible. I'm very thorough with my work and take pride in what I create. One day, am hoping to become a successful freelance artist!
